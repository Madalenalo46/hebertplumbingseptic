Frequently Asked Questions

How many portable toilets do I need for my event?

We recommend one unit per 50 guests for a four-hour event, with adjustments for longer durations or alcohol service.

Do you provide ADA-compliant porta potties?

Yes, we carry ADA-compliant units with wider doors, ground-level access, and handrails.

How often are units serviced during a rental?

Service frequency depends on your rental plan, but most long-term units are cleaned and restocked weekly.

Can restroom trailers be rented for weddings?

Yes, we offer luxury trailers with sinks, lighting, and climate control for upscale events.

Do you deliver on weekends or after hours?

Absolutely. We provide delivery and servicing 24/7 for events and emergencies.

How do I determine the number of restrooms I need?

Our staff evaluates your guest count, duration, and event type to recommend the right number of units.

Bear Creek, AK Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Bear Creek is a census-designated place (CDP) in Kenai Peninsula Borough, Alaska, United States. At the 2020 census the population was 2,129 up from 1,956 in 2010. Bear Creek is a few miles north of Seward near the stream of the same name and its source, Bear Lake.
